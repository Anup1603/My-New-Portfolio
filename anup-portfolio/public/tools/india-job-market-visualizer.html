What does "AI exposure" mean? — "AI का असर" का मतलब?

A 0–10 score for how much of a job today's computers and AI could do, or make much faster. Desk-and-screen work scores high; hands-on work that needs your body or a real person present — farming, cooking, nursing, driving, repairs — scores low.

A high score means the job will likely change, not vanish. Think "how different will this job feel in a few years," not "will it disappear."
